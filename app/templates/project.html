Our Mission

With {% include "modules/template.html" %}, we aim to revolutionize the possibilities of ECG analysis through the use of AI. We understand the intrinsic complexity of ECG time series and are committed to building a community where scientists and professionals collaborate to make optimal use of these data.

By creating this unique platform, we wish to:

  • Promote innovation: Open exchange about current developments in ECG-based AI accelerates scientific progress.
  • Strengthen collaboration: Combining expert knowledge creates synergies that go beyond the capabilities of individual professionals.
  • Increase precision in diagnostics: With optimized AI models, ECG analyses can be conducted more accurately and effectively.
  • Ensure data privacy and security: The entire application can run locally, ensuring that data never leaves the user's computer.
